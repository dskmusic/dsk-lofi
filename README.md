**A pocket lo-fi studio for your music.** Play, remix and export your favorite tracks — right from your phone, no internet required (except for the YouTube tab).

Built by [DSK Music](https://dskmusic.com) — free tools for musicians since 2002.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎚️ Playback & Looping
- Smooth audio engine with **waveform visualization** and zoomable timeline
- Variable playback **speed control**, gapless transitions
- Sleep timer with custom duration or "end of track"

### 🎛️ Effects Rack
- **Reverb** — from subtle room ambience to deep cathedral space
- **Delay** — tempo-aware echoes with feedback control
- **Chorus** — warm modulation for thickness and movement
- **Lo-fi macro** — one slider for instant vinyl/tape character: muffle, saturation, wow & flutter, and crackle
- Custom presets — save and recall your favorite combinations

### 📂 Library & Playlists
- Browse and play any folder on your device
- Create, edit, import and export **playlists**
- Save full **projects** (`.dsk` files) — your tracks, effects, loops and settings, all in one file
- Drag-and-drop reordering, quick search

### 🌐 Online 
- Search directly from the app
- Stream audio-only, or download tracks straight to your library
- Identify songs playing nearby and search them online instantly
- Fast parallel downloads with MP3 conversion built in

### 🎤 Lyrics
- Fetch lyrics from **Genius**, **LRCLIB** or **NetEase**
- View, share, or export as PDF

### 🎨 Personalization
- Multiple visual **themes**
- Full **English / Spanish** interface
- Customizable loading screen, auto-gain normalization (soft/normal/loud)

### 📱 Built for Android
- Native WebView app — fast, lightweight, works offline
- Open audio files shared from any app
- Background playback with media notification controls
- **Auto-update** — get new versions without the Play Store

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Vanilla JS + Web Audio API (no frameworks, fast load)
- Kotlin/Android WebView wrapper
- NewPipeExtractor for on-device online access
- lamejs for MP3 encoding
